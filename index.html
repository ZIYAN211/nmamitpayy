In 2021, NMAMIT Pay made strides in enhancing user convenience. We introduced a mobile app, enabling users to bank on the go. Bill payment options were diversified, including college fees, utilities, and more. Our customer support team grew to provide round-the-clock assistance, solidifying our commitment to excellent service.

2022

In 2022, NMAMIT Pay stands as a comprehensive banking platform. Our services have expanded to include investment options and savings accounts. We continue to prioritize user security, implementing advanced encryption techniques. With a growing user base and a commitment to innovation, NMAMIT Pay has evolved into a trusted partner for all your financial needs.
